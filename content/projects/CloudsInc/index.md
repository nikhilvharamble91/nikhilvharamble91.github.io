This web application enables users to view complete details and activities of their properties while also providing full control over connected home appliances. It allows property owners to monitor, manage, and configure their devices seamlessly. Additionally, users can set up and configure their HUB directly through the application, ensuring smooth integration and centralized management of all smart-home functions.
